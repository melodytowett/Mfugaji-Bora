This platform is where Cattle farmers are connected to market.

One of the greatest challenges farmers face is finding Market for their products
Most of the dairy farmers have no permanent place to sell their milk.
Most of the sell their milk at a throw away price.
Beef farmers also dont have satisfactory market to sell their products
Most of the sell them to local butchers at a very low prices.
Here at Mfugaji Bora we help farmers get connected to different market
where they will choose a market of their own choice.

Dairy markets are:

Kenya Co-operative Creameries Ltd

New KCC is the largest business entity in the dairy industry in East Africa.
Its core business involves the procurement of high-quality raw milk which is then processed,
packaged and marketed as milk and milk products. Its range of quality
dairy products includes fresh milk, butter, cheese, flavoured
and unflavoured long-life milk, fermented milk and milk powder.
Location: Creamery House, Dakar Road, Industrial Area, Nairobi.,
Contacts: 0722 203 668, 020 398 0222
Email: info@newkcc.co.ke.

Brookside Dairy

Brookside Dairy is East Africa’s largest milk processor with operations in more than 12 countries.
It prides itself in a heritage of high-quality milk products and has been at the forefront of
delighting customers with high-quality nutritious dairy products over the years.
It is home to Kenya’s most loved dairy brands, Ilara, Tuzo, Molo Milk and Delamere.
Location: Ruiru.
Contacts: 0722 657 000, 020 235 4677.
Email: maziwa@brookside.co.ke.

The company was established in 2012 and produces, processes and markets milk and dairy products.

Location: Nakuru.
